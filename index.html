About me

My name is Jan Babák, and I have been studying web development at the Faculty of Information Technology at Czech Technical University in Prague . I started programming in high school as an optional subject in 2017. Year after that I took the Introduction to Computer Science course at FIT CTU, where I learned to program simple games in Java, and I realized there, that I want to become a software engineer and decided to study at FIT CTU.

I haven't worked in the field yet, but as a part of my undergraduate thesis, I worked on a real project for the company Jagu s.r.o. I had various student jobs in the past. I spend a year doing market research for agency Ipsos s.r.o. and another year working as a sales assistant at Decathlon a.s.
